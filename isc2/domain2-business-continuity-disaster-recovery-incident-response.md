# ISC2 CC Domain 2 - Business Continuity, Disaster Recovery, and Incident Response

## Status

Completed ✅

## Overview

Domain 2 focuses on how organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from disruptions and security incidents.

## Business Continuity

Business Continuity ensures that critical business functions can continue operating during and after a disruption.

### Examples

* Backup systems
* Alternative work locations
* Emergency response plans

## Disaster Recovery

Disaster Recovery focuses on restoring systems, applications, and data after a disruptive event.

### Examples

* Restoring backups
* Recovering servers
* Rebuilding infrastructure

## Incident Response

Incident Response is the process used to identify, contain, investigate, and recover from security incidents.

### Common Phases

* Preparation
* Detection and Analysis
* Containment
* Eradication
* Recovery
* Lessons Learned

## Backups

Backups help organizations recover lost or damaged data.

### Types of Backups

* Full Backup
* Incremental Backup
* Differential Backup

## Key Takeaways

* Organizations must prepare before incidents occur.
* Business continuity helps maintain operations.
* Disaster recovery helps restore systems.
* Incident response provides a structured way to handle security incidents.
