## What Stood Out

I learned that security is not only about protecting systems from cyber attacks.

Physical security measures such as cameras, locks, badges, and monitoring systems are also important parts of an organization's security program.

## Most Important Lesson

Users should only receive the access they actually need. The Principle of Least Privilege helps reduce risk and limit potential damage.

## How This Connects to My Goals

As someone interested in cybersecurity and Web3 security, understanding access control helps me think about how permissions should be managed in systems, applications, and blockchain environments.
